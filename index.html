Highlighted Grants

Committed to Inspiration and Creativity in Action

The Telling Room

Middle School students visit the nationally recognized creative writing center in Portland to explore the power of creative expression.

KHS Band heads to NYC

KHS Concert Band, Wind Ensemble and Chamber Choir travel to an international music festival in NYC.

Kindergarten Yoga

Kindergarten students receive weekly yoga classes for six weeks. Yoga has been shown to support self-expression, expand imagination and increase concentration.

3D Printers at KHS

Supporting the KHS STEM teachers as they seek to increase the availability of this highly creative technology for students. New classes include AutoCAD 3D Print, drafting and pre-engineering.

Camp Kieve Leadership Program

Every year in October the 7th Grade students have the opportunity to attend the week-long Leadership School at Camp Kieve. Here they learn team building, decision making, communication skills and self-development practices.

Julia Robinson Math Festival

The Julia Robinson Math Festival is a celebration of math attended by all Elementary school students. The event features mathematical challenges, puzzles and other activities in a non-competitive setting.
