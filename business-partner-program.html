Partners in the Education of Future Innovation

The mission of the Education Foundation of the Kennebunks and Arundel is to work with the schools of RSU 21 to inspire and ignite young minds by funding creative and innovative programs for students and professional development for teachers and other staff.

The EFKA accomplishes its mission through grant-making and other initiatives that celebrate learning. Grants for student programs aim to increase students' ability to succeed in the 21st century economy by supporting hands-on learning and cutting-edge ideas.

About EFKA

By engaging the community in the success of our students, we are able to provide creative and experiential learning opportunities for the students and educators of RSU 21.

EFKA is an independent, charitable 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization, governed by an unpaid Board of Directors.
