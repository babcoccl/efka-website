An Endowment for Our Children’s Future

We are establishing an endowment that allows us to plan well in advance and to create a long-term strategic funding vision for what we, the community and our educators can accomplish for the benefit of our students. Our current model of annual funding includes a significant reliance on special event fundraising. Special events have life cycles, are dependent on weather and the availability and skills of volunteers.

An endowment will permit the Foundation to have a profoundly positive impact in enhancing academic excellence for our current and future students.

Investments at the following levels permit the work of the Foundation to continue with a stable source of funds:

$500,000

A gift of this size could fund leadership development programs like sending our 7th graders to Camp Kieve, and providing a Kieve educator in-residence for 7 weeks for all three grades at the Middle School.

$250,000

Funding at this level would allow us to expand our offerings in STEM, including the first ever robotics team at the high school, and a pre-engineering laboratory to ensure pre-college exposure to these disciplines.

$100,000

Support at this level enhances our ability to provide hands-on learning experiences like our gardening and greenhouse project, which provides students with practical, comprehensive farm to table knowledge and understanding.

$25,000 - $50,000

Endowments of this size support a variety of projects in many areas including professional development for teachers and project-based and experiential learning like Destination Imagination, and arts courses.

Charitable Bequests

A charitable bequest is a gift to the Education Foundation of the Kennebunks and Arundel that is made through your will or living trust. While you are alive, you retain complete control of your bequest assets. When your estate is settled, your estate will receive a federal estate tax deduction equal to the value of your gift.

Ways to define a bequest:

  • A gift of a particular amount of money (e.g., $25,000).
  • A gift of a specific item (e.g., 1,000 shares of stock).
  • A gift from the remainder of your estate (residuary bequest) after families and debts are paid.

Sample Bequest Language:

"I hereby give, devise, and bequeath (a dollar amount; or a number of shares of stock; or all or a percentage of the residue of my estate) to the Education Foundation of the Kennebunks and Arundel of Kennebunk in the State of Maine for its general use and purposes."

Bequest Alternatives:

  • Make EFKA a designated beneficiary of a retirement plan.
  • Instruct your bank to "pay on death" to EFKA.
  • Instruct your brokerage to "transfer on death" to EFKA.
